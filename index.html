Local-first, naturally

Your library, right where you use it.

NoctilAI runs on your Windows computer and keeps your roleplay library there too. No cloud storage is required to create, continue or organize your stories.

NoctilAI supports local GGUF models through llama-cpp-python. The technical setup is there when you need it, without becoming the center of the experience.

Ready on your computer Your scenes and characters are saved where you already use them.
Continue offline Once a model is installed, your stories remain available without a connection.
Choose your setup Pick a compatible model that suits your hardware and the way you write.
Move it when you want Export characters and keep clean backups of your own library.
No subscription required The core writing experience does not depend on a hosted AI plan.
