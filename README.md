# NoctilAI

NoctilAI is a local AI roleplay application designed for immersive conversations, character creation and complete privacy.

Unlike cloud-based AI services, NoctilAI allows you to run compatible AI models directly on your own hardware, keeping your data under your control.

## Features

* Local AI model support (GGUF)
* Character creation and management
* Roleplay-focused interface
* Privacy-friendly design
* Conversation history
* Custom AI settings
* Modern dark UI

## Requirements

Requirements depend on the AI model you choose to run.

### minimum :

* Windows 10/11
* 8 GB RAM 
* 4 GB VRAM minimum
* 400 MB (For app only)

### Recommended:

* Windows 10/11
* Dedicated GPU (optional but recommended)
* 16 GB RAM or more for larger models

## Privacy

NoctilAI is designed with local execution in mind. Conversations and character data remain on your device unless you choose to share them.
